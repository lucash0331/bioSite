Kali Sky

Kali Sky is a 28-year-old animal lover from the great state of Georgia. Kali grew up in an upper-class family and has 3 brothers and 2 sisters. Growing up, Kali was always captivated by animals, and this led to her having a dog, snake and a guinea pig as pets. In Kali’s free time you can catch her playing with her daughter, bonding with her puppy or relaxing at the beach. She graduated high school from Buford high school in 2012. After completing high school, she attended Georgia State University, graduating in 2016 with a degree in Journalism. During college Kali worked for WSB-TV Atlanta and WXIA-TV. Shortly after graduating college, Kali gave birth to a beautiful girl named Skyler. After two years of working on camera in the news industry, Kali felt like she was not fulfilling her life’s calling of helping people. First, she started working at the YMCA focusing on guiding troubled youth in the right direction. Even after helping numerous people succeed she still felt a void in her heart, forcing her to find more meaningful work. Shortly after the YMCA, Kali started working for a non- profit organization called Must Ministries. During her time at Must Ministries, Kali raised over 1 million dollars to help feed starving children in the Metro Atlanta area. After a year on the job, Kali came to the conclusion that she wanted to be a first responder. After comparing and contrasting different jobs in that field, Kali knew right away that she wanted to be an Emergency Medical Technician. She attended a brutal 6-month long EMT school at Grady EMS Academy. Currently, Kali is working as a licensed EMT in the Atlanta suburbs and is loving every second of it!
